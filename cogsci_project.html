Welcome to the Cognitive Science Project Participation Form

This study is a side project led by Jun Huang, exploring the intersection between personality types and cognitive abilities, with a focus on facial recognition. I am interested in how different Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) personality types perform on the UNSW Face Test, a measure of facial recognition ability.

Participation involves taking two online tests: the MBTI personality test and the UNSW Face Test. Your participation will help advance our understanding in cognitive science and personality psychology.

Before proceeding, please take the MBTI personality test and the UNSW Face Test.
