Xiangwei HUANG

I am an asistant professor at Shenzhen technology university who interests in investigating the behavior of different novel materials with potential innovative applications.

I received my PhD from École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) in Switzerland. My thesis focused on understanding the rich quantum properties in topological semimetals by studying them under extreme conditions like ultra-low temperature and high magnetic fields.
